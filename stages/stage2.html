stage 2 tumor

what is stage 2

Stage 1 tumors are the earliest stage of true cancer. These tumors are small and if they have spread at all to lymph nodes, the spread is only microscopic. The cancer is small and has only spread a little into nearby tissues. It has not spread to any lymph nodes or other body areas.

symptoms of stage 2 tumors

The symptoms of a brain tumor depend on its size and location in the brain. Some slow-growing tumors may not cause any symptoms at first. Eventually, the tumor may put pressure on the brain, which may cause signs and symptoms such as severe headaches that happen more often and seem more severe, seizures, sensory changes such vision, smell, and hearing, nausea or vomiting.

cure and prevention

Brain tumors are a serious medical condition that can have life-altering consequences. However, there are several preventive measures you can take to reduce your risk of developing brain tumors. In addition to avoiding radiation exposure, wearing protective gear, maintaining a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and avoiding tobacco and excessive alcohol consumption, there are several other steps you can take.Managing stress and getting enough sleep can also reduce your risk of developing brain tumors. Chronic stress weakens the immune system, making it more difficult for your body to fight off cancer cells. Lack of sleep can also weaken the immune system and increase your risk of developing cancer.
